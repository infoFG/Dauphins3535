About

Welcome to Les Dauphins sur-le-parc, a residential condo building where comfort and community meet. Located in the eastern Plateau Mont-Royal in Montreal, facing Parc Lafontaine, our building offers a pleasant living environment and exceptional quality of life.

We have 15 employees who ensure the smooth running of the building and the maintenance of common areas. Five board members manage the owners' association.

Transparency, respect, and quality of life are at the heart of our mission.

Amenities

Residents of Les Dauphins sur-le-parc have access to several common amenities:

  • Heated indoor pool
  • Sauna and steam room
  • Fitness room with basic equipment
  • Community room for gatherings and parties
  • Rooftop terrace with lounge areas
  • Bicycle and seasonal equipment storage

Values

To ensure harmonious cohabitation, certain values are upheld in our building:

  • Respect for quiet hours between 10 PM and 7 AM
  • Proper use of common areas
  • Responsible waste and recycling management
  • Pets allowed under certain conditions

The complete rules are available on CondoWeb.

Neighborhood Life

La Fontaine Park, just steps from the building, is a green lung at the heart of Plateau-Mont-Royal. Here is an overview of what it offers residents.

☀️ Summer activities

  • Paddle boats on the two ponds
  • Picnic areas (tables, gazebos)
  • Tennis courts
  • Pétanque court
  • Children's playground
  • Bike path and walking trails
  • Théâtre de Verdure — free outdoor shows
  • Outdoor chess tables

❄️ Winter activities

  • Outdoor skating rink on the frozen ponds
  • Snowshoeing and cross-country skiing (groomed trails)
  • Sledding hill for children

🎭 Culture & events

  • Théâtre de Verdure — concerts, plays, shows all summer
  • Les Journées Brésiliennes — ljbfestival.ca
  • Haïti en Folie Festival — haitienfolie.com
  • Calixa-Lavallée Cultural Centre — exhibitions, workshops
